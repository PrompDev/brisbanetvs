Our Story

The Rentek Evolution

Brisbane TVs emerged from Rentek, a local IT support firm. The company identified a market gap where premium 4K TVs and Starlink systems lacked proper installation expertise.

Common problems included crooked OLED mounts, poorly positioned WiFi routers, and messy cable management. Brisbane TVs was created to address these issues using IT networking precision in residential settings.

Technicians, Not Handymen

The "No Surprise" Promise

1. We Carry The Stock

The team maintains on-board inventory of heavy-duty, trade-grade mounting options (Tilt, Fixed, Full Motion). No waiting for parts or trips to the hardware store.

2. Fixed-Price Menu
