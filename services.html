Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions?

How much does professional TV mounting cost in Brisbane?

Standard packages start at $275 for screens up to 55 inches (flat/tilt bracket on Gyprock). Larger screens (56-75 inches) cost $385 with heavy-duty bracket.

Do you supply the TV wall bracket?

Yes, we stock trade-grade brackets (Fixed, Tilt, Full Motion) in our vans. No need to pre-purchase from Bunnings.

Can you mount a TV on a metal stud wall?

Yes, using specialist toggle bolts and anchors designed for metal studs, securing up to 80kg.

Do you hide the cables inside the wall?

Yes, cable concealment is available as an add-on (+$99), routing HDMI and power cables behind plasterboard, subject to wall cavity access.

Do you install Starlink on the roof?

Yes, permanent roof installation available for North Brisbane suburbs, including secure bolting and internal data cable routing.
