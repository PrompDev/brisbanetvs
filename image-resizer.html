Bulk image resizer

Drop in any number of images. The tool spits out 1200×1200 and 800×800 .webp versions, center-cropped, filenames preserved. Example: install-ashgrove.jpg → install-ashgrove1200x1200.webp + install-ashgrove800x800.webp.

0.90
Runs entirely in your browser — images never leave your device. · Uses native canvas.toBlob('image/webp') and JSZip.
