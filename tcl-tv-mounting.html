TCL TV Mounting FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions About TCL TV Installation

Can you wall mount a 98-inch TCL?

Yes — we install 98-inch TCLs (C855, C755, P755) more than any other installer in Brisbane. The 98" C855 weighs around 75kg, which is at the upper limit of what residential wall framing can safely support. We always inspect the wall before quoting, send three technicians for the safety lift, and use a steel-backed bracket spanning at least four wall studs. Allow approximately 2 hours for a 98" install with cable concealment.

Will my Gyprock wall handle a 98-inch TCL?

Most modern Brisbane Gyprock walls (post-2010 construction with 90mm timber studs at 450mm centres) can support a 98" TCL when correctly anchored across multiple studs with a heavy-duty bracket. We always physically inspect the wall before installation to identify stud locations and confirm framing depth. For older single-skin or lath-and-plaster walls we may recommend additional reinforcement.

How much does it cost to mount a TCL TV in Brisbane?

Standard TCL installations follow our fixed-price packages: $275 for screens up to 55 inches (C655 55", P755 55"), $385 for 65-75 inch screens (C745, C755, C855), and $550 for 76-85 inch screens. The 98-inch installs are quoted on inspection because they require additional resources — typically $695-$795 for a full installation including cable concealment.

Will the TCL Google TV / Roku setup be done as part of installation?

Yes. Every install includes Google TV setup (or Roku, depending on model), WiFi pairing, and basic streaming app sign-in. We can also configure your account, set up Stan/Netflix/Disney+/Kayo Sports profiles, and pair the TV with your soundbar or receiver before we leave.

Can I mount my TCL TV above a fireplace or air-con vent?

Mounting above a fireplace is possible but we recommend a tilting or full-motion bracket to angle the screen downward for comfortable viewing. Avoid mounting directly above an air-conditioning vent — the temperature differential can affect panel longevity. We assess each location during the booking and offer alternatives if the proposed position isn't ideal.
