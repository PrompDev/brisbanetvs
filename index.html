TV Mounting on Every Wall Type

What Type of Wall Do You Have? We Mount on All of Them

Brisbane homes range from 1900s Queenslander VJ walls to brand-new Hebel and brick veneer construction. We carry the right anchors, drill bits, and brackets for every wall type we encounter.

🗛

Gyprock / Plasterboard

The standard for most Brisbane homes built since 1970. We locate the timber or steel studs and anchor the bracket directly into the framing. Standard $275-$550 packages cover all Gyprock installations.

🪓

Brick & Double Brick

Common in 1950s-80s Brisbane homes. We use specialist masonry drill bits and heavy-duty concrete anchors rated for screens up to 80kg. Add $55 to your standard package for brick wall mounting.

🏗

Concrete & Hebel

Apartment building walls and modern Hebel-clad homes. We use diamond-tipped masonry bits, chemical anchors, and heavy-duty concrete bolts for a rock-solid mount. Quoted on inspection.

🏠

Timber & VJ Panel Walls

Queenslander interiors with VJ pine panelling or original timber lining. We mount directly into the timber framing using heritage-sensitive techniques that preserve the wall's character.

🏢

Fibro & Lath-and-Plaster

Older post-war Brisbane homes. Fibro requires low-vibration drilling and reinforced backing plates. We never anchor into fibro alone — we always locate the studs behind it.

⚙

Metal Stud Construction

Common in newer apartments and townhouses. We use specialist self-drilling metal stud toggles and anchors rated to 80kg per fixing point.

Choose The Right TV Bracket

Fixed, Tilt or Full-Motion: Which Bracket Do You Need?

The right bracket depends on your room layout, viewing angle, and whether you need to swivel the TV. Every package includes the appropriate bracket for your install — here's what's available.

□

Fixed / Flush Mount

Sits the TV closest to the wall (15-30mm gap) for the cleanest, most flush look. Best for living rooms with seating directly facing the screen and no need to angle the TV. Included as standard in all our packages.

▾

Tilt Mount

Allows 5-15° downward tilt to reduce glare and improve viewing angle when the TV is mounted higher than seated eye level. Perfect for bedrooms, above-fireplace installations, and rooms with bright windows. Included as standard.

↺

Full-Motion / Swivel Arm

Extends, swivels and tilts in any direction. Ideal for corner-mounted TVs, kitchens with multiple viewing positions, or open-plan rooms where the screen needs to angle for different seating areas. +$120 upgrade on any package.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About TV Wall Mounting in Brisbane

How much does TV wall mounting cost in Brisbane?

Our fixed-price packages start at $275 inc GST for screens up to 55 inches (bracket included). The Living Room package for 56-75 inch screens is $385, and our Cinema package for 76-85 inch screens is $550. All prices include the bracket, professional installation, and a 5-year warranty. Cable concealment is an optional add-on for $99.

Do you supply the TV wall bracket?

Yes. We carry trade-grade tilt, fixed, and full-motion brackets in our van rated for screens from 32 to 85 inches. The bracket is included in every package price. You can also upgrade to a full-motion swivel arm for an additional $120.

Can you mount a TV on brick, concrete, or VJ walls?

Yes. We mount TVs on all wall types including Gyprock, timber studs, metal studs, brick, concrete, and VJ panel walls. Brick and concrete walls require specialist masonry anchors, which is available as a $55 add-on.

Can you hide the TV cables inside the wall?

Yes. Our in-wall cable concealment service routes HDMI and power cables behind the plasterboard for a completely clean, floating look. This is available for Gyprock cavity walls as a $99 add-on. For brick or solid walls, we offer surface-mounted cable management channels.

What areas in Brisbane do you service?

We service all of North Brisbane and surrounding suburbs within 30km, including Chermside, Aspley, Kedron, Nundah, Wavell Heights, Carseldine, Albany Creek, Bridgeman Downs, North Lakes, Mango Hill, Redcliffe, Sandgate, Strathpine, and many more. Check our service area pages or use the booking form to confirm availability in your suburb.

How long does a TV wall mounting installation take?

A standard TV wall mounting takes approximately 45-60 minutes. If you add cable concealment, allow an additional 30 minutes. Larger 75"+ installs typically take 90 minutes. We can often offer same-day or next-day availability for North Brisbane suburbs.

Do I need a licensed electrician to wall mount a TV?

No, you don't need an electrician for a standard TV wall mounting where you're using existing power and HDMI cables. However, if you want a new power point installed behind the TV (so the power cable is fully hidden), that work must be done by a licensed electrician. We can coordinate this with our partner electrician if needed.

Can you mount a TV above a fireplace?

Yes — this is one of the most common premium installs we do. We strongly recommend a tilting bracket for above-fireplace mounting because the screen sits higher than seated eye level. The tilt allows the TV to angle downward for a much more comfortable viewing experience and reduces neck strain.

Will mounting damage my walls?

No. We make minimal pre-planned holes that are anchored into structural studs — if you ever remove the TV, the holes are easily filled and painted over (smaller than a 10-cent piece). Our zero-mess policy means we use drop sheets, vacuum the dust as we drill, and clean up everything before we leave.

Can you connect my soundbar, Foxtel, PS5 or Apple TV at the same time?

Yes — this is included in the standard service. We connect every device you have (soundbar, Foxtel iQ, Apple TV, Sky/Foxtel, PS5, Xbox, Chromecast, etc.), test that each one is working, set up your input labels on the TV, and tune the screen to display correctly.

Do you offer same-day TV mounting?

Yes, same-day installation is available depending on our schedule. Call before midday on a weekday and we'll usually fit you in that afternoon. Saturday installations book out quickly — book a few days in advance for weekend slots.

Do you offer a warranty?

Yes — every installation comes with a 5-year workmanship warranty. If anything related to our installation work fails within 5 years, we'll come back and fix it free of charge. The bracket itself also carries the manufacturer's warranty (typically 5-10 years).

Are you fully insured?

Yes. We carry $20 million in public liability insurance, all our technicians are police-checked and ABN-registered, and Brisbane TVs is operated by Rentek — an established local IT and AV business. You're fully protected when we work in your home.

What's the largest TV you can mount?

We mount TVs up to 100 inches. The most common big-screen installs we do are 85" and 98" models from Samsung (QN90F), Sony (Bravia 5/9), and TCL (C855, C755, P755). Anything 85"+ requires two technicians and is usually quoted on inspection.
