Brisbane Wall Types

TV Mounting for Every
Brisbane Wall Type.

Brisbane homes span a century of building techniques — double-brick Queenslanders, Hebel new-builds, old gyprock, timber VJ walls. Here's what we do differently on each surface across Brisbane.

Brick

HEPA dust extraction, brick-face drilling, rated dynabolts — never plastic plugs.

Concrete

SDS-plus tooling with sleeve anchors rated well beyond the TV's load.

Gyprock

Asbestos-tested pre-1987, stud-caught where possible, Snaptoggle on cavity runs.

Timber Stud

Pilot-drilled dead-centre. Structural #14 lag screws, pull-tested every fixing.

VJ & Weatherboard

Heritage-safe fixings. Cables routed internal. Zero visible damage.

Hebel / AAC

Dedicated Hebel sleeves, torqued to spec. Standard anchors crumble this stuff.

Double Brick

Cavity-depth scoped before drilling. Anchors that bite the inner skin.

How to Hide TV Cables In a Brisbane Queenslander — Without Cutting Timber ›

Hiding TV cables in a Brisbane Queenslander is nothing like mounting on gyprock. VJ boards are 30mm of solid hardwood. Behind them sits a picture rail at about 1.2 metres — enough for two screws, not a full bracket. Drill it wrong and you split century-old timber. Most DIY guides skip this bit.

We run cables the way Brisbane sparkies have for decades. On high-set Queenslanders we drop through the floor and come up where you want power, HDMI and surround. On low-sets and flats we fish through the wall cavity into a recessed GPO behind the TV. Same-day wall mount installs across Paddington, Ashgrove, Red Hill, New Farm and Bulimba. No split timber. No visible cables. Bracket included, 5-year warranty.

TV Wall Mounting Brisbane — Frequently Asked Questions

Stuck on something? Everything you need to know about TV wall mounting in Brisbane — pricing, wall types, same-day service, and more.

01
Pricing

How much does TV wall mounting cost in Brisbane?

Flat-rate TV wall mounting in Brisbane starts at $149 for TVs up to 55", $229 for 55–75" screens, and $349 for 75–98" panels. Every quote includes GST, a correctly-rated wall bracket, and our lifetime workmanship guarantee. In-wall cable concealment is an extra $100–$150 depending on wall type. No call-out fees, no hidden charges — we quote the job, not the hour.

02
Coverage

Is there a TV mounting service near me in Brisbane?

Yes — Brisbane TVs has mobile installers across 109 suburbs, from inner-city Paddington, New Farm and Teneriffe out to Chermside, Bulimba, Indooroopilly and Wynnum. Most Brisbane postcodes are within a 30-minute drive of an installer, which is why we rank as a top-rated "TV mounting near me" result on Google for homes from West End to the Redlands.

03
Same-Day Service

Do you offer same-day TV mounting in Brisbane?

Yes — same-day TV installation is available for most inner and middle-ring Brisbane suburbs, Monday to Saturday. Book or call before 10 AM and we'll do our best to have an installer at your address that afternoon. Outer suburbs and pre-booked brick or Hebel wall jobs typically go in the next morning.

04
Timing

How long does a Brisbane TV install take?

A standard wall-mount TV install in Brisbane takes 60–90 minutes from knock-on-door to signal test. Brick, Hebel or oversized TVs with in-wall cable runs typically run 90–120 minutes. We book 2-hour arrival windows instead of all-day blocks, so you're not stuck waiting around.

05
Wall Types

Can you mount a TV on brick or Hebel walls in Brisbane?

Yes — brick, double-brick, concrete and Hebel are our specialty. We use SDS-plus hammer drills, HEPA dust extraction and rated masonry anchors (never plastic plugs). We've mounted hundreds of TVs on Brisbane's brick homes including 1920s Queenslanders in Paddington and post-war inner-city terraces in West End and New Farm.

06
Cable Concealment

Can you hide the cables behind the wall?

Yes. In-wall cable concealment routes HDMI, power and AV cables through the wall cavity to a hidden GPO behind the TV — fully code-compliant when done with in-wall rated cabling. Pricing is $100–$150 extra depending on wall type. For rentals or brick walls we also offer low-profile surface-mounted cable covers that paint to match.

07
Wall Integrity

Will wall-mounting damage my walls?

Minimal — we drill 4–8 pencil-sized pilot holes, all concealed behind the TV once mounted. If you ever remove the bracket, they patch and paint in minutes. Stud-finders, laser levels and a two-installer lift on larger TVs avoid the over-drilling that DIY attempts leave behind. Every installer is insured and Brisbane-based.

08
TV Sizes

What size TVs can you wall-mount?

From 32" bedroom TVs all the way up to 98" commercial panels. Anything over 75" is a two-installer job for safety and wall integrity. We supply the correct bracket for your screen — VESA-matched and load-tested well beyond the TV's weight, covering all major brands including Samsung, LG, Sony, TCL and Hisense.

09
Service Areas

What areas of Brisbane do you service?

We cover all of Greater Brisbane including North Brisbane, South Brisbane, Bayside, Western Suburbs, Redlands, Ipswich, Logan and Moreton Bay. Same-day wall mounting is typically available for inner and middle-ring suburbs. Outer-metro postcodes may incur a small $20–$40 travel fee — full Brisbane suburb list is in the Service Areas section above.

10
Fireplaces

Can you mount a TV above a fireplace in Brisbane?

Yes — above-fireplace installs are one of our most-booked jobs. We maintain a minimum 30cm heat-safe clearance above the mantle, fit a tilt or full-motion bracket so the screen drops to eye-level while watching, and route cables out to the nearest GPO for a clean mantle. Gas fireplaces need extra clearance — we'll check your setup on the day.
