## Commercial installs have higher stakes

A screen in a public or commercial space needs to be secure, positioned for the room and tidy enough to pass a fit-out. Cables cannot be left exposed where the public can reach them, screens often need to go higher and heavier than at home, and the work usually has to fit around your trading or office hours. We handle commercial jobs with that in mind — secure mounting, concealed or trunked cabling and minimal disruption.

## What commercial TV installation covers Single or multi-screen From one boardroom display to a wall of screens in a venue or gym, mounted level and consistent. Secure public-space mounting Heavy-duty brackets and out-of-reach positioning suited to public and high-traffic areas. Digital signage & cabling Signage displays mounted and cabled, with trunking or concealment to keep the space clean. Install Process How we handle a commercial job Site assessment of wall types, screen sizes, positions and power/data. Quote with scope, screen count and any after-hours requirements. Secure mounting with brackets rated for the screens and location. Cable management with concealment or lockable trunking for public areas. Testing, sign-off and a tax invoice for your business. Best Suited For Where we install commercial screens

We install for offices and boardrooms, gyms and studios, cafes, restaurants and bars, medical and dental clinics, waiting rooms, retail and showrooms. Multi-screen sports walls and digital menu boards are common requests. Busy precincts like Fortitude Valley , Newstead and Chermside are well within our service area.

## After-hours and low-disruption work

We can work after hours or before opening so your business is not disrupted, and we keep the site clean as we go. For multi-screen jobs we schedule the right crew so the install is completed in as few visits as possible.
