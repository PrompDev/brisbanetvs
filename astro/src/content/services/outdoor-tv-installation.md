## Outdoor installs are not the same as indoor

Brisbane patios get sun, humidity, storms and salt air near the bay. A standard indoor bracket will rust, a normal TV will struggle in direct light, and unprotected cables will degrade fast. We plan outdoor installs around the weather: corrosion-resistant hardware, a position that limits glare, and weatherproofed cabling.

## What outdoor TV installation includes Weather-rated bracket Stainless or zinc-coated bracket and fixings chosen to resist rust in humid and coastal Brisbane conditions. Glare & angle planning We position the screen to limit direct sun and reflection so it stays watchable through the day. Weatherproofed cabling Cables run in suitable conduit and sealed entry points to keep moisture out. Install Process How we install an outdoor TV Assess the location for sun, weather exposure, wall type and viewing angle. Confirm the TV is suitable for the spot, or advise on an outdoor-rated option. Mount with a corrosion-resistant bracket rated for the screen size. Run and weatherproof the cabling, with a power and signal plan. Test the screen and confirm the viewing angle from your seating. Best Suited For Patios, decks, pools and entertaining areas

Outdoor TV installation suits covered patios, alfresco kitchens, pool areas, decks and entertaining spaces. It is popular in bayside suburbs like Sandgate , Brighton and Redcliffe , and in larger homes with dedicated outdoor living areas across North Brisbane.

## Outdoor-rated TVs and warranties

Standard indoor TVs are not built for outdoor heat, humidity or moisture, and mounting one outside can void the manufacturer warranty. For fully exposed areas we recommend a purpose-built outdoor TV. For covered, protected patios an indoor TV may be acceptable — we will give you a straight answer for your specific spot.
