## Wall types and install planning

Ferny Grove's housing stock includes a charming mix of post-war timber homes, 1970s and 80s brick builds, and the contemporary townhouses and apartments of the new Ferny Grove Central precinct. The older homes often sit on elevated stumps, with renovated lower levels featuring modern plasterboard walls. The newer developments near the train station have standard Gyprock on steel framing — consistent and straightforward to mount on.

Every job starts with structure. We locate timber studs where possible, check masonry or rendered walls before drilling, and confirm whether a cavity is suitable for low-voltage cable concealment. If in-wall cable hiding is not right for the property, we recommend a neat conduit or cabinet route instead of making unnecessary holes.
