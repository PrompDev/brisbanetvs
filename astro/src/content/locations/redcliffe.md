## Wall types and install planning

Redcliffe's housing stock is wonderfully varied — from the iconic fibro and timber beach cottages that defined the suburb's holiday origins, to modern waterfront apartments and contemporary family homes. The older cottages often feature hardboard or fibro-cement walls with timber framing, while the apartment complexes along Redcliffe Parade have concrete construction with plasterboard finishes. The suburb's coastal location demands careful hardware selection to resist salt air corrosion.

Every job starts with structure. We locate timber studs where possible, check masonry or rendered walls before drilling, and confirm whether a cavity is suitable for low-voltage cable concealment. If in-wall cable hiding is not right for the property, we recommend a neat conduit or cabinet route instead of making unnecessary holes.
