## Wall types and install planning

Everton Park homes are a real mix: original post-war timber-and-fibro houses, heavily renovated family homes, and newer townhouse complexes off Stafford Road and South Pine Road. The older houses often combine hardboard or fibro linings with newer plasterboard in the renovated sections, while the townhouses are typically standard Gyprock on timber or steel studs. That variety means the wall surface should always be checked before any bracket goes up.

Every job starts with structure. We locate timber studs where possible, check masonry or rendered walls before drilling, and confirm whether a cavity is suitable for low-voltage cable concealment. If in-wall cable hiding is not right for the property, we recommend a neat conduit or cabinet route instead of making unnecessary holes.
