## Wall types and install planning

Newstead living is dominated by apartments and townhouses, with some commercial and live-work spaces around Gasworks. Walls are typically concrete or masonry party walls with a plasterboard or metal-stud lining, which means the strongest mounting point is often the concrete behind the lining. We sound out the wall first, then choose between cavity fixings and concrete anchors so the TV is held by real structure.

Every job starts with structure. We locate timber studs where possible, check masonry or rendered walls before drilling, and confirm whether a cavity is suitable for low-voltage cable concealment. If in-wall cable hiding is not right for the property, we recommend a neat conduit or cabinet route instead of making unnecessary holes.
