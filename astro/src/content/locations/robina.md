## TV mounting in Robina

Brisbane TVs provides professional TV wall mounting, bracket supply, cable concealment and soundbar installation for customers in Robina. The area includes modern townhouses, apartments and rendered brick homes, so we confirm the wall construction, TV size, bracket type and cable plan before the job is booked.

A good install starts with the wall, not the television. Our installers check for studs, masonry, concrete, steel framing or apartment building restrictions before drilling. That matters for larger screens, full-motion brackets, Samsung Frame TVs and living rooms where the television needs to sit level with cabinetry or a soundbar.

## Wall types and install planning

In Robina, most jobs are either Gyprock cavity walls, brick or masonry, rendered walls, or apartment walls with concrete or steel framing behind the plaster. Each one needs a different fixing method. We bring timber screws, masonry anchors, stud finders, levels and cable-routing gear so the install is secure and tidy.

For cable concealment, we first check whether the wall has a safe cavity and whether power needs an electrician. If the wall is masonry or a body-corporate wall, we recommend slim conduit, a cabinet route or another neat external cable path rather than making unnecessary holes.
