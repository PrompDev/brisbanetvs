## Wall types and install planning

Kedron's housing stock is a beautiful mix of Queenslanders, post-war homes, and contemporary builds. The older character homes along Leckie Road and Kedron Brook Road feature VJ walls and high ceilings that create stunning backdrops for wall-mounted TVs. The newer builds and renovations typically use standard Gyprock on timber or steel frames, which is straightforward for our team to work with.

Every job starts with structure. We locate timber studs where possible, check masonry or rendered walls before drilling, and confirm whether a cavity is suitable for low-voltage cable concealment. If in-wall cable hiding is not right for the property, we recommend a neat conduit or cabinet route instead of making unnecessary holes.
