Many Newport homes have dedicated media rooms or large open-plan living built for 75 inch and bigger screens, which we reinforce across multiple studs.

Every job starts with structure. We locate timber studs where possible, check masonry or rendered walls before drilling, and confirm whether a cavity is suitable for low-voltage cable concealment.
