## Wall types and install planning

Wooloowin's housing is predominantly character homes — Queenslanders, workers' cottages, and art deco residences that have been beautifully maintained or renovated. Internal walls vary from original VJ timber to modern Gyprock, and many homes feature a combination of both where renovations have extended the original footprint. The suburb's apartment stock along Lutwyche Road adds modern concrete and Gyprock surfaces to our installation repertoire.

Every job starts with structure. We locate timber studs where possible, check masonry or rendered walls before drilling, and confirm whether a cavity is suitable for low-voltage cable concealment. If in-wall cable hiding is not right for the property, we recommend a neat conduit or cabinet route instead of making unnecessary holes.
