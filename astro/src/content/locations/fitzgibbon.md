## Wall types and install planning

As a relatively new suburb, Fitzgibbon's housing stock is almost entirely modern construction. Homes feature standard Gyprock on timber or steel frames, open-plan living areas, and well-designed entertainment spaces. The uniform construction quality across the suburb means our installations here are consistently efficient and the results are always clean and professional.

Every job starts with structure. We locate timber studs where possible, check masonry or rendered walls before drilling, and confirm whether a cavity is suitable for low-voltage cable concealment. If in-wall cable hiding is not right for the property, we recommend a neat conduit or cabinet route instead of making unnecessary holes.
