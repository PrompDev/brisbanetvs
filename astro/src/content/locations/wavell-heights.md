## Wall types and install planning

The suburb features predominantly post-war brick and timber homes, many of which have been extensively renovated and extended over the decades. Wavell Heights homes often have large living areas with high ceilings, which provides excellent wall space for larger screens. The mix of rendered brick feature walls and Gyprock-lined rooms means we encounter a variety of mounting scenarios across the suburb.

Every job starts with structure. We locate timber studs where possible, check masonry or rendered walls before drilling, and confirm whether a cavity is suitable for low-voltage cable concealment. If in-wall cable hiding is not right for the property, we recommend a neat conduit or cabinet route instead of making unnecessary holes.
