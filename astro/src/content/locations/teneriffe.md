## Wall types and install planning

Teneriffe living is dominated by warehouse-conversion apartments and modern premium units. Walls are frequently solid brick or concrete, sometimes with exposed face brick as a design feature, and the buildings often have firm rules about drilling. The upside is that once anchored correctly, these masonry walls hold a large screen better than almost any other surface in Brisbane.

Every job starts with structure. We locate timber studs where possible, check masonry or rendered walls before drilling, and confirm whether a cavity is suitable for low-voltage cable concealment. If in-wall cable hiding is not right for the property, we recommend a neat conduit or cabinet route instead of making unnecessary holes.

## Install tip for Teneriffe

Exposed brick walls in Teneriffe woolstores look fantastic with a TV mounted on them, but drilling into heritage brick should be planned to land in the brick face, not the mortar joints, for maximum hold. We mark and pre-drill carefully, and check your building's rules on fixings before we start.
