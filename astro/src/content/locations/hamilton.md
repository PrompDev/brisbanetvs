## Wall types and install planning

Hamilton's housing is divided between grand heritage homes on the hill, established brick homes in the lower streets, and premium apartment complexes along the river. The heritage homes feature a variety of wall types including VJ timber, lathe-and-plaster, and modern plasterboard in renovated sections. The luxury apartments at Portside and Hamilton Harbour typically have concrete walls with plasterboard finishes, requiring specialist concrete anchoring for secure TV mounting.

Every job starts with structure. We locate timber studs where possible, check masonry or rendered walls before drilling, and confirm whether a cavity is suitable for low-voltage cable concealment. If in-wall cable hiding is not right for the property, we recommend a neat conduit or cabinet route instead of making unnecessary holes.
