## Wall types and install planning

The housing in Chermside West is largely comprised of well-maintained brick and timber homes from the 1960s through to the 1980s, with many having undergone significant renovations. Internal walls are typically Gyprock on timber studs, which is our preferred mounting surface. Some homes in the suburb back onto parkland and have been extended with modern additions that include purpose-built media rooms.

Every job starts with structure. We locate timber studs where possible, check masonry or rendered walls before drilling, and confirm whether a cavity is suitable for low-voltage cable concealment. If in-wall cable hiding is not right for the property, we recommend a neat conduit or cabinet route instead of making unnecessary holes.
