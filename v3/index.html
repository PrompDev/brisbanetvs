TV Wall Mounting Brisbane — Frequently Asked Questions

Stuck on something? Everything you need to know about TV wall mounting in Brisbane — pricing, wall types, same-day service, and more.

How much does TV wall mounting cost in Brisbane?

Flat-rate TV wall mounting in Brisbane starts at $149 for TVs up to 55", $229 for 55–75", and $349 for 75–98" panels. Prices include GST, the bracket, and a lifetime workmanship guarantee. In-wall cable concealment is an extra $100–$150. No call-out fees, no hidden charges — we quote the job, not the hour.

How long does a Brisbane TV install take?

Most standard installs take 60–90 minutes from knock-on-door to signal test. Brick, Hebel, or oversized TVs with in-wall cable runs typically take 90–120 minutes. We book in 2-hour windows so you're never waiting around all day.

Can you mount a TV on brick or Hebel walls?

Yes — brick, double-brick, concrete, and Hebel are our specialty. We use SDS-plus hammer drills, HEPA dust extraction, and rated masonry anchors (never plastic plugs). We've mounted hundreds of TVs on Brisbane's brick homes including 1920s Queenslanders and post-war inner-city terraces.

Do you offer same-day TV mounting in Brisbane?

Yes — same-day service is available for most inner and middle-ring Brisbane suburbs. If you call or book before 10 AM, we'll do our best to be at your place that afternoon. Outer suburbs and pre-booked brick-wall jobs usually go in the next morning.

Will the mounting damage my walls?

Minimal — we drill 4–8 pilot holes the size of a pencil, concealed behind the TV. If you ever remove the mount, they patch and paint in minutes. Our stud-finders, laser levels, and two-installer lift on larger TVs avoid the over-drilling most DIY attempts leave behind.

Can you hide the cables behind the wall?

Yes. In-wall cable concealment routes all cables through the wall cavity to a hidden power outlet behind the TV — code-compliant when done with the correct in-wall rated cabling. Costs $100–$150 extra. For rental or brick walls we also offer low-profile surface-mounted cable covers.

What areas of Brisbane do you service?

We cover all of Greater Brisbane including North Brisbane, South Brisbane, Bayside, Western Suburbs, Redlands, Ipswich, Logan, and Moreton Bay. Same-day service is typically available for inner and middle-ring suburbs. Outer suburbs may incur a small $20–$40 travel fee. See the full suburb list above.

What size TVs can you mount?

From 32" bedroom TVs all the way up to 98" commercial panels. Anything over 75" is a two-installer job for safety. We supply the right-rated bracket for the screen — VESA-matched and load-tested well beyond the TV's weight.
