Samsung TV Mounting FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions About Samsung TV Installation

Do you install Samsung's Slim Fit Wall Mount for The Frame?

Yes — The Frame comes with the Slim Fit Wall Mount in the box, and we install it as part of the standard package. We level the bracket to within 1mm using a laser, plan the One Connect Box cable route, and ensure the TV sits perfectly flush against the wall. No additional charge for the Slim Fit installation.

Where do you put the Samsung One Connect Box?

Most customers place it inside the TV unit or media cabinet directly below the TV. For wall-mounted TVs with no furniture below, we can install a small wall-mounted shelf or a recessed wall enclosure. The 5m One Connect cable that ships with The Frame and S95F gives us flexibility — we discuss the options on arrival.

Can you wall mount a 98-inch Samsung QN90F?

Yes. The 98" QN90F weighs around 78kg without a stand. We bring two technicians for the safety lift, use a heavy-duty bracket rated for at least 90kg, and reinforce the mounting points across multiple wall studs. We've installed several 98" Samsungs across North Brisbane — book in advance as we need to schedule the two-tech crew.

Does the Samsung S95F OLED need a special bracket?

No special bracket is required, but the panel is extremely thin (~11mm) and fragile. We use a standard 300x300mm VESA bracket for the 55"/65" and a 400x400mm for the 83". Care during handling is the most important factor — we always do a two-person lift and never apply pressure to the centre of the panel.

Can you set up SmartThings, Bixby and Apple AirPlay on my new Samsung TV?

Yes. Every installation includes basic tuning and WiFi setup. For an additional fee we can complete full SmartThings configuration, Apple AirPlay pairing, soundbar Q-Symphony setup, and account sign-in for streaming services like Netflix, Stan, Disney+, and Kayo Sports.
