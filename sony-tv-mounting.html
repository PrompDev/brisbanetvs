The 5 Sony Bravias Brisbane Customers Mount Most

1. Sony Bravia 9 (Mini-LED Flagship)

Sizes (Australia): 75", 85"

VESA pattern: 300x300mm

Weight: 42 kg (75"), 58 kg (85")

Type: Mini-LED with QLED quantum dot — Sony's brightest LCD ever

Brisbane installation tip: The 85" Bravia 9 is one of the heaviest TVs sold in Australia. The chassis is deeper than a comparable Samsung QN90F, but the bracket interface is standard. We always check stud spacing first — for 85" we need to span at least three studs with a heavy-duty backing plate. The TV's deeper profile means a slim flush-mount bracket can still leave 80mm air gap, so for a true wall-hugging look we recommend the standard fixed mount and a slimmer media setup below.

2. Sony Bravia 8 OLED

Sizes: 55", 65", 77"

VESA pattern: 300x300mm across all sizes

Weight: 17 kg (55"), 22 kg (65"), 34 kg (77")

Type: WOLED with Acoustic Surface Audio+ — the panel itself vibrates to produce sound

Brisbane installation tip: The Bravia 8 is unique — the panel is the speaker. Most brackets attach to the rear chassis without issue, but cheap third-party brackets that contact the panel surface can mute or distort the audio. We exclusively use brackets that anchor to Sony's reinforced rear mounting points. The panel is also OLED, so the same fragility rules apply: two-person handling, no centre-panel pressure, microfibre-padded prep surface. Cable management is clean — the rear has a recessed channel that hides HDMI runs neatly.

Sony TV Mounting FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions About Sony Bravia Installation

Will wall mounting affect my Sony Bravia 8's Acoustic Surface speaker?

Not when it's done correctly. The Acoustic Surface Audio+ system uses the panel itself as the speaker, so brackets that contact the panel surface can mute or distort the sound. We always use brackets that anchor to Sony's reinforced rear mounting points only — the same way Sony does it in their showroom installations.

Can you mount a Sony A95L QD-OLED safely?

Yes — we install A95L panels regularly in Brisbane home theatre rooms. The QD-OLED panel is extremely fragile (thinner than 11mm in places), so we use mandatory two-person handling, padded prep surfaces, and the highest-grade tilt or fixed bracket. We never apply pressure to the centre of the panel during the install.

How heavy is the 85-inch Sony Bravia 9 and can my wall handle it?

The 85" Bravia 9 weighs around 58 kg without the stand. Most Brisbane homes built since 2010 have 90mm timber-stud walls at 450mm centres, which can handle this weight when we span at least three studs with a reinforced backing plate. For older fibro or single-leaf brick walls we may recommend additional bracing or a steel-backed bracket. We always inspect the wall before quoting on Bravia 9 installations.

Do you set up Google TV, Apple AirPlay, and Bravia Cam on Sony TVs?

Yes. Every Sony installation includes Google TV setup, WiFi pairing, and basic streaming app sign-in. For an additional fee we can complete full smart home configuration including Apple AirPlay, Bravia Cam (where supported), Google Assistant pairing, and Sony Bravia Connect app setup.

Can you wall mount a Sony Bravia and connect a PS5 at the same time?

Absolutely. PS5 setup is one of the most common requests for Sony Bravia installations, especially the Bravia 8 OLED and Bravia 9 which have Sony's Perfect for PS5 features. We mount the TV, route the PS5 HDMI cable through the wall (with cable concealment), enable Auto HDR Tone Mapping and Auto Genre Picture Mode, and verify 4K/120Hz output is working before we leave.
