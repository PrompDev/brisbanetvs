Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 May 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you book our TV mounting services, we collect personal information including your name, phone number, email address, and service address. This information is necessary to provide and coordinate your installation.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Schedule and confirm your TV mounting appointment
  • Send you SMS and email booking confirmations and reminders
  • Respond to your enquiries and provide customer support
  • Improve our services based on feedback

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share necessary details with our installation technicians to fulfil your service booking.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

6. Your Rights

Under Australian Privacy Law, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@brisbanetvs.com.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@brisbanetvs.com or call 1300 312 271.
